# Hi, I'm Dhrumin Thakkar

## Chief Architect | Enterprise Modernization | Cloud | Data & AI

I help organizations modernize complex enterprise platforms by bringing together architecture, cloud, data engineering, AI, integration, DevOps, and practical delivery governance.

My work focuses on designing platforms that are scalable, secure, observable, and maintainable beyond the first release. I work across enterprise modernization, cloud strategy, data platforms, AI enabled solutions, integration architecture, and engineering enablement.

I enjoy turning complex business and technology problems into clear architecture direction, practical delivery models, and technology foundations that teams can build on with confidence.
